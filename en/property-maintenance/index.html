FAQ

Before you get in touch

I live abroad. Can you handle everything remotely?

Yes. Most of my English-speaking clients are owners who live abroad. We agree the work on WhatsApp, I arrange access, and I send photos of the property before and after the work.

Can you quote from photos?

For straightforward jobs, usually yes: send 3 or 4 photos, the location and the approximate size. If access, waste or terrain raise doubts, I will visit the property first.

Is green waste removal included?

Grouping the waste inside the plot is included. Removal with loading and transport is quoted separately and agreed before the work starts.

Can you keep the property maintained all year?

Yes. After the initial clean-up we can agree regular visits so the garden or plot never becomes overgrown again, with photos sent after each visit.
